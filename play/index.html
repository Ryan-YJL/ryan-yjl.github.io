Top 30 Hits

"Top 30 Hits" is built with productivity in mind — To show you the top 30 music hits and let you listen to all in a single play.

It saves you time from looking at the chart and searching on all over to have a listen or wait for that top 10 hits episode on MTV.

The site is optimized for laptop screens and larger but is also user-friendly for smaller screens.
The reason why the chart is concised to an area is to allow the user to get all the information he/she needs in a one glance without having to scroll down with fancy lazy loading.

Chart data from MTV and Billboard, official video sources from Youtube is crawled and aggregated automatically using PhantomJS and nodeJS.

Data is saved in MySQL server and relayed as JSON feed for the minisite.
